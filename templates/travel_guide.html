Travel Guide

Getting There

The venue is located at Sunset Gardens, 1234 Wedding Lane, Hometown, ST 56789. The closest major airport is Hometown International (HTI), about 30 minutes from the venue.

Transportation Options

  • Ride Shares: Uber and Lyft are widely available in the area.
  • Rental Cars: Available at HTI Airport with all major providers.
  • Valet Parking: Complimentary at the venue.

Nearby Hotels

  • The Garden Inn – 0.5 miles from venue
  • Hometown Suites – 1.2 miles away
  • Airbnb Options – Plenty of nearby homes available

Things to Do

If you're staying for the weekend, here are some fun local activities:

  • Explore Hometown Botanical Gardens
  • Walk the historic downtown area
  • Take a wine tour at Sunset Vineyards
