Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony starts at 4:00 PM. We recommend arriving by 3:30 PM to allow time for parking and seating.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, we suggest "Garden Formal" — think light suits, dresses, and semi-formal wear suitable for an outdoor setting.

Can I bring a guest?

If your invitation includes a plus one, feel free to bring a guest! If you're unsure, please reach out.

Will transportation be provided?

No, guests should arrange their own transportation. There will be complimentary valet parking at the venue.

Is the ceremony outdoors?

Yes — weather permitting. Please dress accordingly and bring sun protection if needed.
